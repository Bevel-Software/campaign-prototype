# WELLPASS Product Positioning & Messaging Master

# **Why positioning matters?**

---

> "Positioning is the process of defining how your product solves a problem better than alternatives for your specific audience. Positioning is about comparison and defining how you fit into the market for prospects and customers."
> 

→ Internal Story alignment across different departments is crucial to achieve external market clarity. 

# Difference between **Positioning**, **Messaging** & **Copywriting**

---

- **Positioning** is *where* your product fits in the market, *who* you serve, *what* you’re doing, and how you’re *different*.
- **Messaging** is the clarity of *what* you say about your product.
- **Copywriting** is the cleverness of *how* you communicate it all.

### **Brand Positioning ([FRONTIFY](https://brand.egym.com/document/162#/intro/this-is-wellpass)):**

> EGYM Wellpass is the leading corporate fitness network, empowering companies to promote employee health and well-being with a single, flexible membership. With access to 19,000+ premium fitness and wellness facilities across Europe & North America, Wellpass connects employees to gyms, yoga studios, swimming pools, climbing centers, and more—all under one seamless subscription.

Beyond physical locations, Wellpass offers a comprehensive digital platform with thousands of online workouts, live classes, and guided meditations, ensuring employees can train anytime, anywhere.

With 660,000+ active members from 18,000+ companies, Wellpass helps organizations foster a healthier, more engaged workforce while supporting a preventative approach to healthcare. Backed by EGYM’s cutting-edge fitness technology, Wellpass delivers measurable health benefits, driving real impact for both employees and employers.
>

### **Wellpass vs. direct competition**

- vs. **USC**
    - **B2B focus**: Unlike USC, we are B2B-native → our product solution focus on the needs of corporates (e.g. HR portal, payroll integrations, reporting tools)
    - **🎯 Why Us?** Urban Sports Club is great for city people, but **Wellpass is for your entire workforce.** With nearly double the network size, we cover the commuters, the rural home-office workers, and the sales team on the road—not just the city dwellers. Plus, our 'Unlimited' model is fair and inclusive, unlike their complex S/M/L packages.
- vs. **Hansefit**
    - **Partner network**: Unlike Hansefit, our partnership offering extends beyond physical locations to include digital apps for mental health and nutrition, providing a comprehensive solution for overall well-being.
    - **🎯 Why Us?** Hansefit is a traditional BGM provider, but Wellpass is a **health-tech partner**. While they focus on prevention courses and basic access, we provide a data-driven health platform. Our app doesn’t just open doors; it tracks BioAge, visualizes progress, and engages employees digitally—driving the high participation rates you need for ROI.
- vs. **Wellhub**
    - **Pricing**: Unlike Wellhub, we offer a simple and transparent pricing model for corporates where you only pay one membership per employee, not complex packages.
    - **🎯 Why Us?** Wellhub is a global aggregator, but Wellpass is the **local specialist.** Their tiered pricing model creates friction—employees have to pay more to access better gyms, which discourages usage. We offer a flat rate for access to premium gyms that Wellhub locks behind expensive upgrades. We are simpler for members, fairer to partners, and locally denser.
